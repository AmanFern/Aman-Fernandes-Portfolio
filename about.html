About Me

Hello there! My name is Aman Fernandes, and I am a first-year Creative Computing student at Bath Spa University. I am passionate about my art and have been doing it as a hobby since I was a child. I hope that my creativity will help me with my future endeavours in design and development. I am quite new to web development and coding in general but I have learnt a lot in this year. I've used all the techniques I've learnt in class, and explored the internet for new techniques to make this website as attractive as possible.
